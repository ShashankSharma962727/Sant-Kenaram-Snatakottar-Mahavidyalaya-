Admissions

Join our institution and embark on your journey towards academic excellence and
personal growth.

Admission Process

1

Get Application Form

Obtain admission form from college office or download online.

2

Fill Application

Complete the form with accurate information and attach required documents.

3

Submit Form

Submit the application form with fee at the admission counter.

4

Merit List

Check merit list on college notice board and website.

5

Document Verification

Appear for document verification as per schedule.

6

Fee Payment

Pay the admission fee and collect your admission slip.

Eligibility Criteria

B.A. / B.Sc. / B.Com

10+2 or equivalent from a recognized board

B.Sc. Agriculture

10+2 with Science stream (PCM/PCB)

M.A. / M.Sc. / M.Com

Graduation in relevant subject from recognized university

B.Ed.

Graduation with minimum 50% marks

Required Documents

10th Marksheet & Certificate

12th Marksheet & Certificate

Graduation Marksheet (for PG courses)

Character Certificate

Transfer Certificate

Migration Certificate

Caste Certificate (if applicable)

Income Certificate (for scholarship)

Domicile Certificate

Passport Size Photographs (4 copies)

Aadhaar Card
