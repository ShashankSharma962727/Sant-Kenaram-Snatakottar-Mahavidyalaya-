संत केनाराम स्नातकोत्तर महाविद्यालय

Sant Kenaram Snatakottar Mahavidyalaya, located in Lodi, Robertsganj, Sonbhadra (Uttar Pradesh), is a distinguished institution of higher education dedicated to academic excellence and holistic development of students.

Affiliated to Mahatma Gandhi Kashi Vidyapith, Varanasi, our college offers a comprehensive range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Arts, Science, Commerce, Agriculture, and Education.

Our institution stands as a beacon of knowledge in the Sonbhadra region, providing quality education to students from diverse backgrounds and helping them achieve their academic and professional aspirations.

Our Vision

To be a premier institution of higher education that fosters intellectual growth, ethical values, and social responsibility. We envision creating leaders who contribute positively to society and drive progress in their respective fields.

To provide quality education accessible to all, nurture academic excellence through innovative teaching, promote research and scholarly activities, and develop well-rounded individuals prepared for the challenges of the modern world.
